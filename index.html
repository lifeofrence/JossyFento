About Jossy-Femto Limited

Our Company

Jossy-Femto Limited was incorporated as a Limited Liability Company under the Companies and Allied Matters Decree. We are dedicated to providing top-tier consultancy services, research and development, and specialized engineering solutions in areas such as computer engineering, electrical engineering, communications, instrumentation engineering, Civil engineering, project management, and supply chain management, mobile solution, I.T audit, renewable Energy solution, Digitization services and Automation.

Our Mission

Our primary mission is to elevate the quality of services in Nigeria to standards obtainable in more developed countries by applying high standards and fresh ideas to improve the industry.

Our Vision

We aim to project a formidable team capable of leading our foreign contemporaries in service deliveries both at present and in the future.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive solutions tailored to meet your project needs with excellence and precision.

Mobile Solutions

We provide technology, services, and software designed specifically for mobile devices. Our solutions include mobile applications, payment systems, enterprise solutions, and device management, helping businesses and individuals stay connected and efficient on-the-go.

I.T Audit

We conduct comprehensive evaluations of information systems, management controls, and IT infrastructure. Our audits ensure system security, data accuracy, and reliable operations, helping organizations maintain robust IT governance.

Renewable Energy Solutions

We specialize in sustainable energy solutions including solar powered streetlights, hydropower plants, renewable energy microgrids, and solar power systems. Our solutions help reduce dependence on fossil fuels while providing reliable, eco-friendly power.

Digitization Services

We transform physical information into digital formats, enabling better data storage, access, and processing. Our services improve efficiency, enable remote work, and facilitate digital transformation while preserving crucial information.
