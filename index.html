About Jossy-Femto Limited

Our Company

Jossy-Femto Limited was incorporated as a Limited Liability company under the company and allied matters decree to provide Turnkey Services in the Design, Building, and Construction Engineering, Project Management, and Supply Chain Management.

Our Mission

Our primary mission is to elevate the quality of services in Nigeria to standards obtainable in more developed countries by applying high standards and fresh ideas to improve the industry.

Our Vision

We aim to project a formidable team capable of leading our foreign contemporaries in service deliveries both at present and in the future.

Management Team

The company is run by dynamic professionals who have served in various capacities in both public and private sectors. The staff strength includes qualified engineers, Quantity Surveyors, Cost Control Analysts, Project Managers, and Builders with over 35 years' experience in infrastructure development and management.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive solutions tailored to meet your project needs with excellence and precision.

Training Services

We offer comprehensive training programs tailored to enhance skills in engineering, project management, and construction. Our courses are designed by experts with extensive industry experience, ensuring practical knowledge for career growth and efficiency.

Full Turnkey Solutions

We provide end-to-end project solutions from design to completion, managing every phase including planning, execution, and handover. This ensures seamless delivery, meeting client specifications with high-quality standards and timely completion.

Construction

Our construction services cover residential, commercial, and industrial projects. With a skilled workforce and advanced techniques, we deliver durable structures that adhere to international safety and quality standards, tailored to client needs.

Procurement

We manage the acquisition of materials and equipment, ensuring cost-effectiveness and quality. Our procurement process involves sourcing from reliable suppliers, negotiating contracts, and delivering materials on time to support project timelines.

IT Equipment

We supply a range of IT hardware including computers, servers, and networking devices. Our products are sourced from leading manufacturers, ensuring reliability and performance for businesses seeking to upgrade their technological infrastructure.

Supply and Maintenance

We provide ongoing supply of construction materials and equipment, paired with maintenance services. Our team ensures regular upkeep and repairs, extending the lifespan of assets and maintaining optimal operational efficiency.
